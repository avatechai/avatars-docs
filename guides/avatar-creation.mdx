## Create avatars

You can generate avatars from [AvatarLabs](https://labs.avatech.ai). With the help of text-to-image AI model, you can create different avatar by only describing your imagination.

After generated your avatar, you can click the 'view code' button at the top-right corner to copy the avatarId. Then replace the avatar id inside the `useAvatar` hook.
