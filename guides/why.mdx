title: "Why Avatars is Important"
---

Inspired by the developers' creativity of how LLM agents can be, the SDK is designed with the 2 observations in mind.

To make your avatar alive, three different type of services is needed:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Large language model">
    We support [Langchain](https://python.langchain.com/) or [Vercel AI](https://sdk.vercel.ai/docs) to generate novel human-like content for your avatars.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Avatar providers" >
    We support [Live2D](https://www.live2d.com/en/) or [Rive](https://rive.app/) **mouth-sync supported** avatar.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Voice providers">
    A Text to Speech voice generation providers is needed. These voice provider will generate voices based on the text input, so that avatars can respond through speech.
    Currently we support [Elevenlabs](https://beta.elevenlabs.io/) to generate the voice of your avatars.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

1. Uesful Agent is context-aware and dynamic
  - Switching styles, voice and LLM models should be as easy as switching api.
  - Everything is standardised with appropriate modular abstraction for the avatar. 
  - innovation in various domains of avatars are stackable and reusable. 
2. User expect instant feedback and have
  - as such, we design it to be fast, responsive, and programmable in minute details.
  - we provdie off-the-box animation solution while agent is using different tools and components. 
  - We abstract the complexity of building avatars across styles
